# Initiatives

Initiatives collect forward-looking design and research directions that extend
AXIS beyond the currently implemented systems. They are intentionally more
exploratory than the core manuals and tutorials, but they are still written
to stay technically concrete and useful for engineering follow-up.

## Available Initiatives

| Initiative | Description |
|---|---|
| [Neural Submodules in AXIS](neural-submodules/index.md) | Exploration of how neural modules, online learning, and PyTorch-based execution could be integrated into AXIS systems without abandoning the framework's explicit mathematical structure. |
