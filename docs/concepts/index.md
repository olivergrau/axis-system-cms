# Concepts

This series covers the mathematical foundations and design philosophy behind
AXIS agents. It is aimed at researchers and developers who want to understand
the theory before (or alongside) the code.

## Reading Order

| # | Document | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | [AXIS Vision](00-axis-vision.md) | Vision statement and research goals |
| 1 | [Biological Inspiration](01-axis-cms-vision.md) | Biological inspiration, design principles, modeling strategy |
| 2 | [Math as Modeling](02-math-as-modeling.md) | Why mathematical formalism; alternatives and trade-offs |
| 3 | [Agent Framework](03-agent-framework.md) | Generic 8-tuple agent framework |
| 4 | [System A](04-system-a.md) | Hunger drive, modulation, softmax policy, energy dynamics |
| 5 | [System A+W](05-system-aw.md) | Curiosity drive, world model, drive arbitration, reduction property |

Documents are numbered and designed to be read in order. Each builds on the
concepts introduced in the previous one.
